About Kumpulan Bumikon

Over 30 years of pioneering excellence in Facility Management and Construction

The Prologue

At Kumpulan Bumikon, our mission is to deliver sustainable, pioneering, and world-class solutions in the Facility Management and Construction sectors. We are unwavering in our commitment to the highest standards of integrity, safety, and environmental stewardship. Our aspiration is not merely to satisfy but to consistently surpass client expectations through operational excellence, perpetual innovation, and a people-first philosophy, thus forging enduring value for the communities we serve and the industries we shape.

Our History

With over 30 years of distinguished excellence, Kumpulan Bumikon was founded in the 1990s as a modest initiative by a team of passionate professionals. From these humble beginnings, we have evolved into a trusted industry vanguard, serving both government entities and private corporations. Our journey is hallmarked by strategic expansion, technological advancement, and the successful realization of landmark projects. Today, we are revered for our unwavering reliability, technical mastery, and collaborative success across Malaysia.
